ARSO is currently looking for sales-focused business development help while staying open to practical builders, operators, and project-based collaborators.

Open Contract Role

Business Development & Solutions Associate

Flexible 1099 contract role • Base + commission

ARSO Solutions Lab is looking for a motivated, sales-focused person to help build relationships, identify business opportunities, and introduce local businesses to practical systems, automation, dashboards, and AI-assisted solutions.

This role is ideal for someone who enjoys networking, talking with business owners, learning technology, and spotting operational problems that ARSO can help solve.

Responsibilities

  • Build relationships with local businesses and organizations
  • Identify operational pain points and improvement opportunities
  • Generate leads and schedule discovery conversations
  • Represent ARSO professionally in sales and networking conversations
  • Help introduce ARSO services and products, including OccuBoard
  • Explain software, automation, CRM, dashboard, and AI concepts in a non-technical way

Good Fit

  • Strong communication and relationship-building skills
  • Comfortable discussing technology and AI with non-technical business owners
  • Basic computer knowledge and willingness to learn new tools
  • Interested in business operations and how better systems save time
  • Self-motivated, reliable, organized, and professional
  • Recent graduates are encouraged to apply

Compensation & Structure

  • 1099 contract role
  • Flexible schedule
  • Base compensation plus commission
  • Remote-friendly with local networking opportunities
